ATX USB Hub

This is a 4 port USB hub powered by ATX PSU or external 5V source. There are 6 USB ports available. 2 are for power only and have a separate on/off switch (right USB ports looking from front side). Left and middle ports are always powered and are connected to USB hub. This way you can power your RaspberryPi and extend it's USB ports from single circuit. You can download PDF schematics HERE

Known issues

Version 1.0 has a wrong ATX connector layout. So you can either use the onboard 3.3V regulator and apply +5V power supply externaly. Or you can solder ATX connector to the board via wires.

Holes for ATX connector are too small.
